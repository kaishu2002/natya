GITA GOVINDAM

The Gita Govinda has been studied and cherished across time by Sanskrit scholars and enthusiasts but enjoyed only in fragments by the common people because artists and artistes have always tended to highlight and popularize only a few sections, based on their personal preferences. As a result, the complete masterpiece is a mystery, very few people have ever had access to. This fascinating prospect of showcasing for the world the complete charm of a literary work was what brought together Guru Dr Pali Chandra and her team supported by Natyasutra - Invis team, transcending differences of language, time zone and country. The programme has been designed based on Natyasastra and Abhinaya Darpana so that the students of all dance forms can make use of this content in their respective forms.
