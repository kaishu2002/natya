Dhanasree Thillana

The Thillana usually consists of a rhythmic sequence that alternates between intricate footwork and graceful upper body movements. It begins with a short prelude that introduces the raga and tala, followed by a series of rhythmic patterns (korvais) that showcase the dancer's prowess.

Narumugaye

Narumugaye” is often associated with the deity Lord Krishna and is sung in praise of his divine qualities and playful nature. The term "Narumugaye" can be translated to convey endearment, often reflecting the feelings of a devotee towards Krishna.
